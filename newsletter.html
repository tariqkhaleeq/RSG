Newsletter


Following the trend of all life sciences, Bioinformatics has become a specialist science. Researchers (and especially graduate students) working in a certain field often have a hard time keeping up with literature in their discipline, let alone following the progress in other sectors. To help fill this gap, we pick three current research papers from a different sector of Bioinformatics and summarize them here once a month.
