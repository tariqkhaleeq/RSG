Welcome to the Regional arm of the iSCB Student Council - Regional Student Group Germany




We aspire to be a platform where young computational biology students and researchers in Germany can network with each other.

If you are interested in joining our network, being an active part in the RSG Germany or if you just want to keep up to date on the latest RSG Germany news, please check out our Events or follow us on Twitter.

We hope to see you at one of the upcoming events!!!
